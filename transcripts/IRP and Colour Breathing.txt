Track 5 - Dreaming of wanting to walk each day…
Wanting to want to walk…
Use this deliberate daydreaming track if you’d love to find yourself wanting to go for a walk each day.
If possible, listen to it as you lie in bed as soon as you wake up.
This track should feel easy and enjoyable. If it doesn’t feel good as you listen to it, stop the track and use Track 4 instead. It can take a little focus to feel optimistic about daydreaming. That’s completely normal!
Come back to the track again tomorrow and see how it feels to you then.
If wanting to walk more isn’t a desire for you, just skip this track.
