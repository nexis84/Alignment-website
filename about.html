About Amanda

I am an alignment coach.
In other words
I help people smile more!

Aligning emotions with the Law of Attraction
is my specialism, following the teachings of Abraham-Hicks.

A Cambridge science graduate, with 30 years' experience in teaching/school leadership, a certified coach since 2007, I am trained in EFT (Emotional Freedom Technique) and Clean Language, Systemic Modelling.

My approach to coaching has been forged in the fires of contrast.
I left my 30 year career with a body inflamed by stress. ('Burnt out' - literally!)
I was sore, lost and worried about money.

But I had my darling husband beside me. Together, we'd make it through.

Four months later, my soulmate was diagnosed with terminal brain cancer.
Four months after that, Michael died, at home, in his favourite room, in October 2021.

Grief was the final focuser.
I had the theory and training.
Bereavement made me do the work.

As I focused daily, reaching for relief, I discovered the power of the work.
Theory became practice.
Hoping became knowing.

As I grew my own smile,
my body healed,
my skills were honed,
and clients came to work with me.

I worked with the diagnosed depressed,
I worked with the dying,
I worked with entrepreneurs under pressure.
Together, we reached for
more enjoyment
more confidence,
expanding success
in alignment with ease...

I worked with teachers and parents, widows and coaches...
I trained groups to carry the alignment work forward...

And now the work continues...
founded in joy,
soaked in the certainty that
wherever we are
we are always in
the perfect place
to reach for
A little more joy!
