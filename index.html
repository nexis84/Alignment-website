My Story

I know what it feels like to search — for years, even decades — for a life that feels truly yours. I left a 30-year career with my body inflamed by stress. Then my darling husband was diagnosed with terminal brain cancer. I nursed him through four months until he died, peaceful and happy, at home.

His dying sharpened everything. Alignment had never mattered more. After he passed, I kept aligning — working through grief, day by day, reaching for a little more joy each time.

That practice brought me out of the darkness and into a life I truly love. Beauty surrounds me. I do what I love. I love what I do. Loss gave me clarity, meaning, and purpose — and I know how to show you the same path.

If you're struggling, I see you. I hear you. I feel you. You deserve to live a life you truly love.
